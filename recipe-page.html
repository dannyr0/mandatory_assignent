#1 Bender's most shiny recipe

BOOZY BROWN
LENTIL SOUP

20 minutes 230 calories Beginner

Ingredients

r

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Warm the olive oil in a large Dutch oven or pot over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is shimmering, add the chopped onion and carrot and cook, stirring often.
  3. Add the garlic, cumin, curry powder, and thyme. Cook until fragrant while stirring constantly.
  4. Pour in the lentils, broth, and the water. Add 1 teaspoon salt and a pinch of pepper.
  5. Transfer 2 cups of the soup to a blender.
  6. Add the chopped greens and cook for 5 more minutes.
  7. Serve while hot. Leftovers will keep well.
  8. 1 large can (28 ounces) diced tomatoes, lightly drained
  9. 1 cup brown or green lentils, picked over and rinsed
  10. 4 cups vegetable broth
  11. 2 cups water
INGREDIENTS
  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow or white onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, pressed or minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • ½ teaspoon dried thyme
